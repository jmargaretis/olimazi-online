Method Effects

I started Method Effects after buying a 2017 Audi S3 and deciding to create a first-to-market front aero splitter for that model year. I bootstrapped the brand, scanned the car, coordinated the CAD work, sourced manufacturing in Southern California, and shipped the finished product worldwide.

The work included fitment constraints, OEM mounting points, custom tooling and packaging, ecommerce, customer support, logistics, and content for Facebook, Instagram, and enthusiast forums.

Process notes and lessons
  • Used vehicle scanning and professional CAD support to turn the initial idea into manufacturable geometry.
  • Designed around fitment, visibility, minimal modification, and an OEM-like appearance.
  • Retooled an expensive aluminum buck after rejecting a gap in the RS3 design; later learned MDF could have reduced that cost.
  • Built custom boxes and shipping processes for a large product moving domestically and internationally.
  • Faced pandemic-era plastic costs and overseas copying of product designs and images.

A finance tracker you own

Built with AI in days, not months—and designed to keep the books in your hands.

This began with managing my mother’s rental property. She does not use a computer, so I wanted a low-friction way to organize records, surface missing information, and prepare cleaner Schedule E figures.

Instead of another bookkeeping subscription, the tracker lives in local files the user owns. Its math is deterministic and costs nothing to run. The same AI assistant that helps keep the books can also work across the rest of life, without trapping the records inside a single-purpose service.

The rental workflow is tested and still being refined. A Schedule C version will adapt the same local-first approach for small-business bookkeeping.

Recipe experiment

Bougatsa — Greek custard pie

I love desserts, but making them myself gives me more control over sugar and ingredients while teaching me the techniques behind them. Bougatsa is part of a larger goal: understand the three primary custard styles well enough to make them deliberately rather than simply follow instructions.

Why it belongs here

Recipes are also a way to share something with family. They make a useful test for saving the original source, recording substitutions, drafting a post, and keeping the personal reason intact instead of producing generic food content.
